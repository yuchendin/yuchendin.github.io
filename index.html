During my studies at UCR, I worked hard to explore various fields of sociology, from classic theories to contemporary social issues, which fascinated me. My passion for sociological research inspires me to continue to study and think deeply, hoping to contribute to understanding and solving the challenges facing today's society

Sociology can help you understand various social phenomena. Studying sociology enables you to master methods and techniques for studying social issues.
